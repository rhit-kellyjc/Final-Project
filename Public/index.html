Welcome to Pomodoro Timer!

Pomodoro timer is an immersive studying experience. Have an exam coming up, loads of homework due soon, or even just want to sit down and tackle some items on your to-do list? Sit down, hit play on our lofi beats and start our timer to begin the Pomodoro technique.

How it works:

The play button under the timer will start the timer. The Pomodoro technique consists of 25 minutes of deep work followed by a 5 minute break (by hitting the short break button!). Do this 4x, then take a long break (by hitting the long break button!).

Add a to-do to the to-do list by clicking the "Add To-Do" button. The maximum amount of to-dos supported is 10, so be sure to check your to-dos off once you've completed them!

Future features

Some features currently being implemented are: a theme button, which will allow users to change the background image of the website to a live image of their choosing (city, beach, mountains, etc). We are also working on adding analytics to allow users to track their to-dos completed, hours studied, and pomodoro sessions completed! Finally, we are working on making our app cleaner on smaller screens like mobile devices.
